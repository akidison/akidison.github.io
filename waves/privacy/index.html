Waves — Dark Mesh Chat

Privacy Policy · Effective: March 21, 2026

Overview

Waves is a peer-to-peer mesh messenger that works without internet. Your privacy is our core principle. We collect as little data as possible.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No accounts — no email, no phone number, no registration
  • No message content — messages are never sent to any server
  • No user profiles — your name and avatar stay on your device
  • No contacts — your friend list is stored locally only
  • No analytics — no tracking, no telemetry, no usage data
  • No advertising — no ads, no ad networks, no ad identifiers
  • No browsing history — no web views, no cookies

What We Store Locally on Your Device

  • Your display name and avatar (UserDefaults)
  • Your contacts/friends list (UserDefaults)
  • Chat messages — stored temporarily, auto-deleted based on your TTL setting (default: 1 hour)
  • Voice messages — stored as temporary audio files, auto-deleted with messages
  • App settings (notifications, TTL, max hops)
  • E2E encryption private key (iOS Keychain — never leaves your device)

All local data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

What We Store in the Cloud

Waves uses Firebase Firestore only for location-based group pins. When you create a location group, we store:

  • Group name
  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
  • Group radius in meters
  • Whether the group has a password (boolean)
  • Password hash (if set) — we never store the actual password
  • Creation date

We do NOT store: who created the group, who joined, any user names, any device identifiers, any messages, or any personal information.

Encryption

All direct (private) messages are encrypted end-to-end using Curve25519 key exchange and ChaChaPoly symmetric encryption. Only the sender and recipient can read message content. Relay nodes in the mesh network see only encrypted data.

Broadcast messages are not encrypted — they are intended for everyone nearby.

Mesh Network

Waves communicates directly between devices using Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework (Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Direct). No data passes through our servers. Messages may relay through intermediate devices to extend range — relay devices cannot read encrypted content.

Permissions

  • Bluetooth — Required to discover and connect with nearby devices
  • Local Network — Required for Wi-Fi Direct mesh communication
  • Microphone — Only when you record a voice message (push-to-talk)
  • Location — Only when you create a location-based group (optional)
  • Photos — Only when you send a photo message (optional)
  • Nearby Interaction — For AR Radar distance measurement (iPhone 11+, optional)

Children's Privacy

Waves is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Data Retention

Messages auto-delete based on your TTL setting (15 minutes to 2 hours). Location group pins remain in Firebase until the group creator deletes them. All local data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Waves uses Firebase Firestore (Google) solely for storing location group coordinates. No user-identifiable data is sent to Firebase. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
