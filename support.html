Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the app or contact us with details about your device and iOS version.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any purchase.
